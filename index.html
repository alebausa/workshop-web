What will you do at the web dev bootcamp?

Module 1

You will learn the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and you will learn a lot of JavaScript. By the end, you will even develop a little game.

Module 2

You will learn how to build databases and program a backend, combine the frontend and the backend and finally you will develop a fullstack application.

Module 3

You will learn ReactJS, one of the most popular JavaScript frontend libraries. You will again develop a fullstack application and will be ready for your next job challenge!
